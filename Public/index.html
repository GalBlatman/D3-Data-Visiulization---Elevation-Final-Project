This simple force-directed graph shows investment connections between Venture Capital firms based in Israel. A physical simulation of charged particles and springs places related VCs in closer proximity, while unrelated VCs are farther apart.

Doubleclicking on a node will fade out all the nodes and links that aren't connected to the node you've doubleclicked on.

Also, you can search for specific VCs using the search option on the top right (don't worry, it has a dropdonw menu to help you find the right one).

This real world data consists of 109 VC firms which exsisted from 1983-2012. The data regards 5435 seperate investments 66.31% of them were made with more than 1 partner.

As seen in the graph, The Israeli VC market is highly syndicated. All but 2 VCs are clustred in a tightly knit pack of investments.
